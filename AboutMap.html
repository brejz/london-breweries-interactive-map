About Interactive Map

The purpose for this map us to give beer enthuists an in-depth view of the type of breweries and brewpubs which are currently available in London. This map also provides insights from various resources, allowing the user to filter by beer type or type of brewery.

Defining Breweries

A brewery or brewing company is a business that makes and sells beer. The place at which beer is commercially made is either called a brewery or a beerhouse, where distinct sets of brewing equipment are called plant.

A Brewpub is an abbreviated term combining the ideas of a brewery and a pub or public-house. A brewpub can take the form of a pub or restaurant that brews beer on the premises.
